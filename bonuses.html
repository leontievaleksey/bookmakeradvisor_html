What you need to know about Bonuses

Many UK bookmakers offer various bonuses to entice players to sign up, as well as offering bonuses for players who are regular punters. Sky bet have their weekly Ј5 bonus and Bet365 do a similar thing giving regular bonuses for loyal players. However cash is not the only type of bonus that is offered by the online bookmakers.

When you are looking for the best bookmaker to open an account with, it pays to take a bit of time to research who offers what bonus, what the amount is and what are the rules of playing through that bonus.

A lot of bonus offers look great and very enticing, but many bonus bets have a clause that means you have to play through your initial deposit a number of times before you can withdraw any funds from a winning bet you have used in conjunction with a bonus offer.

Their are different types of bonuses that bookmakers give and some of them are very attractive. Most online bookmakers now have loyalty programmes where they reward players with bonus bets and other non cash bonus elements.
