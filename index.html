How do we value bookmakers?

With so many bookmakers and sports betting sites online today, it is very difficult to "sort the wheat from the chaff”.

When surveying punters, one of the key questions we got repeatedly was who is the best UK bookmaker? Which bookmaker can we trust? Which bookmaker offers the best bonuses? Which bookmaker offers the best odds.

Here at Bookmaker Advisor, we are always searching for the best opportunities for punters and analyzing various bookmakers. We now have nearly 50 online bookmakers in our review sections and we also have an in depth analysis of each bookmakers bonus offer.

If you look at the UK market then you will always see the top companies to the fore when it comes to self promotion. Companies like Bet365, Ladbrokes, Corals (last 2 now teamed up) William Hill and also the king of bookmaker TV ads...Paddy Power.

These of course are the companies everybody knows and trusts, but what about some of the unheard of companies that also have very solid reputations, companies like: Genting Bet, Mr Green, Racebets, SMARKETS and BetEast.

On Bookmaker Advisor we do all the research so you can find these companies and make a sound decision if they fit with your own betting strategy.

Make sure to check out our individual bookmaker reviews for in depth information about each one. Also check out our individual bonus bet reviews for every UK bookmaker.

How do we pick bonuses and promotions?

All bookmakers have some type of sign up offer to entice players to join and bet with them. This is great, because if you are smart it can really pay off.

At Bookmaker Advisor we have taken a lot of time to research these bonuses right down the finest detail. We have looked at the terms and conditions, and let us tell you, not all bonus offers are what they seem. They may look attractive but many have very strict rules as to how long the bonus is available for, what is the minimum odds you must bet at with your bonus, how many times you have to play through your deposit before you can make any withdrawal of funds.
